
Hallucination Detector Guide

This personal guide explains what AI "hallucinations" are, how to detect them, and practical ways to reduce them when using Claude or other large language models. It is aimed at learners and power users who want simple, copy-paste prompts and clear test cases.

Contents:
- `01-what-is-hallucination.md` — definition, how hallucinations happen, and a simple analogy.
- `02-types-of-hallucinations.md` — the three main types with concrete examples.
- `03-detection-strategies.md` — four practical strategies with copyable prompts.
- `04-my-test-cases.md` — two hands-on tests (Approach 1 vs Approach 2) and a comparison method.

Use these files to build your own "Hallucination Detector" repo and adapt the prompts to your workflows.
