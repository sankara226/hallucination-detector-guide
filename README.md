Hallucination Detector Guide

This small personal guide explains what AI "hallucinations" are, how to spot them, and how to reduce them when using Claude or other LLMs. It is written for learners and power-users who want a practical checklist and simple test cases.

Contents:
- `01-what-is-hallucination.md` — short definition, mechanism, and a simple analogy.
- `02-types-of-hallucinations.md` — the three main types with concrete examples.
- `03-detection-strategies.md` — four practical strategies with copyable prompts.
- `04-my-test-cases.md` — two hands-on tests (Approach 1 vs Approach 2) and an analysis.

See the linked files for quick how-tos and example prompts. Feel free to copy this repo and adapt the prompts to your own workflows.
